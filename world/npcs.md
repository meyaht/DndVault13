- Appearance: Experienced. Shortbow, curved blade. Cut on forearm from Waterdeep, healed.
- What she wants: to understand what she signed up for. The assignment has shifted and nobody told her why.
- Speech: direct, professional, not many wasted words. Asks questions when she thinks she'll get answers.
- Behavior: watches Aldric more than she watches the others. Doesn't comment on it.
- What she knows that the party doesn't: her grandmother was a Fourteenth Watcher. She knows the name but not what it means. Her handlers asked about the Keeper specifically before the party left Waterdeep.
- Current status: at the ridge with Mira and the horses. Approach party.

- Speech: quieter than Sera. Follows her lead.
