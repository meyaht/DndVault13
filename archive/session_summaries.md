## Act 1 — Revel's End (Days 1-3)
Party hired separately by Lyra Ashveil to infiltrate Revel's End and extract a location from Prisoner 13. Used Pass without Trace to enter, reached the cell, and extracted Korda — a dwarf who had refused to reveal a key's location to two factions for three years. On the cliff ledge outside the prison, Korda revealed what she knew: a plain iron key, hidden at the Lusty Mermaid tavern in Targos, opens a vault beneath Waterdeep. Two powerful factions had searched for it nearly a decade. Korda asked to travel with the party. Chugguh agreed. They reached Caer Konig, rested at Regis Halfpenny's shop. Party decided not to deliver to Lyra until they understood what they were delivering.
Threads resolved: Revel's End infiltration complete.

---

## Act 2 — The Road and the Coalition (Days 4-15)
On the road south, the party encountered three Harper riders — including Eryn, a deep cover operative who had spent three years inside the second faction. Mirt (a Lord of Waterdeep, covert) was already traveling with them. In Longsaddle, the coalition formed: party, Harpells, Harpers, Mirt, Lyra (cooperative under pressure), Cassin (flipped), Pellun (cipher operator, cooperative for a written guarantee). The third faction leader was named: Serevak, a Magistrate of the Lords' Alliance who had sat on the committee overseeing Harper Northern operations for eleven years. Mirt had lost two people to Serevak's intelligence. Eryn and Aldran rode for Waterdeep at midnight, carrying the full evidence package to Thessaly (Arcane Brotherhood). Aldric the bard (grandson of Aldric Moonwhisper) turned Korda's testimony into a song that was in three towns before Lyra reached Longsaddle. The second faction's Harpell estate assault was stopped — fourteen riders, no party casualties.
Threads resolved: Coalition formed. Evidence delivered. Song released. Serevak identified.

## Act 3 — Waterdeep and the Seal (Days 15-23)
The key had already moved from Targos when the party arrived on Day 18 — a faction rider had taken it. Davan recruited in Targos as guide: knew Serevak's streets and the building above the vault. Party rode for Waterdeep immediately, arrived dawn Day 22. One day until the convergence window. Four fights through the building above the vault that night: entrance, stairwell, landing, ritual chamber. Seal held. Ritual broken. All four fights complete. Party at level 4 through these fights; leveled to current state through Acts 3-4 development. Serevak arrested before dawn Day 23. Nine prisoners sorted. Thessaly's office, formal lunch, Aldric Moonwhisper (grandfather, woken from 312-year sleep) joined party. The Keeper released from vault binding when Aldric accepted custody of the 14th vault.
Threads resolved: Serevak in custody. Seal intact. Second faction dismantled. Vault/seal arc CLOSED.

---

## Act 4 Setup — Road to Anauroch (Days 23-83)
Post-Waterdeep recovery and travel. The red tome (12 vault locations) in Nameroc's possession revealed the Pillar of Adamant in Anauroch. Nameroc identified that the annotations are deliberately offset ~2 degrees — not error. Zhentarim provided three riders for the approach: Sera, Dav, Mira. Sera's grandmother was a Fourteenth Watcher. Party leveled through this travel period. Party arrived at ridge overlooking Pillar on Day 83 evening. Something moved inside 3 nights ago. Approach planned for full dark tonight.
Threads resolved: Route established. Zhentarim contracted.
