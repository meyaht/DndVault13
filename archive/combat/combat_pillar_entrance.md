## KEY INTELLIGENCE GATHERED

- 13 vaults made for the compact. Others exist naturally.
- Order of vaults was deliberately obscured. Order only matters at the final door.
- Each vault holds a piece required to open the final door (knowledge, keys, words, seals).
- The cult has a copy of the red tome but NOT the index (held only in this vault).
- Index shows which pieces are held where — critical intelligence advantage.
- Cult is partway through collecting pieces. Not at the beginning, not at the end.
- Cult sent the Wraith to clear this vault. It failed. They will send something else.
- Strategy: deny the cult even ONE piece — one missing piece seals the final door forever.
